🎮 Gravity Chess Rules:
• All pieces except pawns fall due to gravity
• White pieces fall down to rank 4, Black pieces fall up to rank 5
• Pawns act as floors - pieces stop falling when they hit pawns
• Pieces can stack on each other in the same column
• Pawns promote to queens when reaching the opposite end
• Capture the enemy king to win! 👑
